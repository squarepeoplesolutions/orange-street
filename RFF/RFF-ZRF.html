RFF London & the Zesh Rehman Foundation Equalities & Diversity cup tournament

On the 31st of August, RFF London and the Zesh Rehman Foundation held an Equalities & Diversity Cup tournament. The tournament was made up of four five a side female teams. It was held at Bellingham Leisure and Lifestyle centre on the 3g pitch and was followed by a female empowerment workshop exploring issues such as identity and discrimination. Our #Achievers supported, planned and assisted in delivery of the tournament. The #Achievers Youth Leader’s Programme aims to encourage young people to take part in a variety of positive youth initiatives that are designed to boost life skills, aspirations, and social action initiatives.

Thanks to all the young people that attended and our partners at the Zesh Rehman Foundation for making it possible! We hope to see even more females involved in community sports in the future.
