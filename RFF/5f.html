Platform 5

Platform 5 is a new youth-led digital platform providing a space for young people to collaborate creatively and showcase their talent.

Featuring fresh, current, forever changing content, from young people for young people, and encouraging people to work together on creative collaborations and projects.

Bringing together a host of professional mentors and brands to nurture young talent, provide tech literacy opportunities and pathways to achievement within the tech, creative and digital media industries.
