Invest in Youth

Invest in Youth Work was a youth-led social action campaign ran in collaboration with Change.org.

The campaign, backed by our Patron Rio Ferdinand, empowered young people from our Lewisham, Southwark and Lambeth programmes to launch an ‘Invest in Youth Work’ campaign to convince London Mayoral candidates to create more youth programmes across the city.

A huge success, the campaign received a range of media coverage and support across the city and from over 40,000 supporters on social media. It also led to an ongoing relationship with Mayor Sadiq Khan and was instrumental in the formation of the Cross Party Commission on Serious Youth Violence lead  by our partners Active Communities Network.
