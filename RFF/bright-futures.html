Bright Futures

Our ‘Bright Futures’ programme ran from 2015 to 2017 across London operating out of local community hubs such as Lewisham’s The New Generation (TNG) Community Centre.

Working in collaboration with the local youth service and funded by BT Supporters Club, the initiative provided youth crime intervention solutions and positive pathways to education, training and employment for young people aged 11 – 19 years old.

This programme saw us develop our training package and was instrumental in facilitating some key partnerships across London enabling us to help thousands of hard-to-reach young people. Through the programme we were able to identify some extremely talented young people who went on to become volunteers and members of our Youth Leaders steering panel who are still involved in shaping our ongoing and programmes.
