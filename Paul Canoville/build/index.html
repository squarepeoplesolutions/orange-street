Paul Canoville Foundation

Building positive futures

Founded by former Chelsea FC footballer Paul Canoville, the Foundation works with young people facing economic, physical or mental adversity to build life-skills, resilience and support networks to build a better future for themselves and their community.

Our mission is to inspire and equip young people to overcome challenges.
