Youth coaching

As part of our commitment to young people we support their development to become sports coaches who can lead the next generation of young people in their community.

We partner with a range of sport for development charities and training organisations to deliver a tailored programme for young people. This includes, the VRQ in Developing Community Activities for Youth at-risk, FA Coaching badges which include safeguarding and first aid together with youth work qualifications. We aim to inspire and equip young people to build a community and peer led response to resilience and support for other young people. This peer to peer work is vitally important in building community relations and developing young people as positive role models in their communities.
