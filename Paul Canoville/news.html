Update – Dallas Golf Tournament

As many of our US supporters will be aware, Paul Canoville has recently been admitted to hospital for a serious operation, and is expected to have an extended period of recovery. Paul is out of hospital now, but will not be able to undertake travel or public appointments in the immediate future.

We have, in conjunction with our local organising committee, taken the decision that as Paul is unable attend we are cancelling the November charitable golf tournament in Dallas – and will reschedule for March 2018. We apologise for any inconvenience and hope we see you all on the new date once confirmed.

A message from Paul Canoville:

I was really looking forward to our Foundation Dallas Charity Golf event on 3rd November 2017 and meeting friends and supporters of the Foundation, old and new.
Unfortunately I fell ill some weeks back and was hospitalised, and although I am recovering now my supporters want me to be 100% recovered before undertaking public events, so we've now decided to reschedule the date in March.
Hopefully this won’t inconvenience our US supporters too much and I look forward to seeing you then.
Thanks as ever for your best wishes and support.
