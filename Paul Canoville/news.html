New Platinum Supporters Club Announced!

Paul Canoville is thrilled to be launching the Foundations' new Platinum Supporters Club, designed to engage you as our supporters and sponsors of our work supporting young people in London and beyond.

With two tiers of membership - one for individual supporters and businesses who wish to support our work and engage Paul in their business networks, and one for Chelsea FC supporters clubs who want a formal relationship with Paul and the Foundation - the Platinum Supporters Club has some exciting opportunities for everyone.
