Zesh Rehman Foundation

FOOTBALL DRIVING SOCIAL CHANGE

The Zesh Rehman Foundation uses football to drive sports participation, community cohesion and social development.

Founded by the professional footballer and Pakistan captain Zesh Rehman in 2010, the Foundation works in the heart of communities to support young people, bring communities together and offer development opportunities within the world of sport.
