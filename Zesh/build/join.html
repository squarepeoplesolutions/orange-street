WE ARE RECRUITING

JOB TITLE: Sport for Development Internship

SALARY & BENEFITS: £10,000 per annum, ongoing training & 30 Days Paid Annual Leave

JOB DESCRIPTION

Zesh Rehman Foundation (ZRF) have an opportunity for a sports coaching professional to join its small team. Supporting ZRF and partnership programmes, our intern will lead on coaching sports sessions for young people across London. As part of the 12-month internship, ZRF will provide training and mentoring to develop you as a sport for development professional with a view to you taking on a project coordination and leadership role in future & growing ZRF’s work in London.

This is a demanding but very rewarding full-time internship which could kick start a professional career in the sport for development sector.

DUTIES AND ACCOUNTABILITIES

  • Plan and deliver sport and youth activities.
  • Support project staff to prepare for sessions and deliver the programme’s activities
  • Support project staff in monitoring and evaluating the success of the projects activities
  • Assist project staff in general tasks such as ordering kit and equipment and booking facilities
  • Adhere to employers and placement organisations Health and Safety, Child Protection and Equalities policies
  • Be available for evening and weekend work
  • Complete various training course e.g., Award in Education and Training, Sports Leaders Tutor Training, Child Protection & Safeguarding.
  • Attend all work based sessions, meetings and training courses on time
  • Adhere to their placement organisations policies around presentation, dress code and representing the organisation externally

PERSON SPECIFICATION

  • Passionate about sport, fitness or physical activity
  • An understanding of sport and young people
  • Self-motivated to learn and improve
  • Good communication skills and ability to relate to other people
  • Ability to engage young people positively
  • To be able to adapt to different challenges in a positive way
  • Willingness to travel to and from sessions on a daily basis
  • Reasonable level of personal fitness
  • FA Level one, other multi-sport qualifications
  • Willingness to become qualified in Sports Leaders and various other tutor training

GENERAL DUTIES INCLUDE

In carrying out the above the post holder will:

  • Keep abreast of developments in services and legislation for young people relevant to the tasks and responsibilities
  • Seek to improve his/her own performance, contribution, knowledge, and skills and participate in training and developmental activities as required
  • Ensure the implementation the Diversity and Equality policy statement
  • Comply with Health and Safety policies and procedures

JOB TYPE: Full time internship

HOW TO APPLY: Email your CV and a brief covering letter outlining your suitability for the role to info@zeshrehmanfoundation.org by 5pm on Thursday 26th October.
