PLAY THE GAME

The ZRF recognises the progression of young people and communities is a central component of what we do. The ‘Play the Game’ programme used sport as a pathway and platform to:

  • Increase sports participation amongst 11 - 25 year olds
  • Offer educational and coaching qualifications to young people to strengthen their chances of securing future employment
  • Equip communities with a higher level of self-sufficiency whereby they can build on the foundations
  • Encourage participation in sport and educational qualifications amongst minority ethnic communities
  • Provide opportunities for young people to progress into competitive mainstream football by working closely with local authorities, Football Associations and Chartered Standard Clubs
  • Provide on-going mentoring to develop young people further
  • Provide pathways in further education and training
