LOCAL GOALS

The Local Goals programme brings a mix of sporting activities to Tower Hamlets' most marginalised young people (aged 14-21), largely of BME backgrounds whom were not accessing any sporting activities. ZRF uses a range of sports to engage these young people, breaking down cultural barriers, and improving community relations and cohesion.

As part of this Sport England funded programme, we utilise coaches as mentors for young people and offer a personal and social development package for them. This, together with access to NGB sports coaching training, coaching and youth work skills, equips young people with the skills they need to run their own community based sports programmes for their peers and the next generation.
